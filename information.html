FOBISIA math competition information

British International School Hanoi




BIS Hanoi is located on Hoa Lan Road, Vinhomes Riverside, in the Long Bien District of Hanoi, Vietnam. This prestigious British International School offers a high- quality education in a suburban setting, just a short drive from Hanoi’s bustling city centre. Visit the website at www.bishanoi.com for more details

BUSES




The competition fee covers transportation both ways between hotels and BIS for both days, and to and from dinner on Friday. Schools will also be transported to and from the airport for their flights.



Meal




Within the competition fee, the following meals are covered; 2 lunches at BIS Hanoi, 1 celebration dinner, snacks and refreshments. We will collect dietary requirements from schools once registration is complete.





Technology & Device




We recommend that students do not bring devices. We will not be using them during the competition. If students do bring their devices, BIS Hanoi will not liable for any loss or damage.



Uniform Expectation





During the event, students will be wearing either their school PE kit or their FOBISIA Primary Competition t-shirt. This will help to easily identify students who are taking part in the competition.

For the celebration dinner and the closing ceremony, students should have their full school uniform.

Finance




At the discretion of trip leaders, please feel free to bring some spending money with you to Hanoi. It would be helpful to carry money in a mix of Vietnamese dong (VND) in small denominations.





Weather




In March, Hanoi experiences warm and pleasant weather, with daytime highs averaging around 24-28°C (75-82°F) and cooler evenings around 18-20°C (64-68°F). Occasional light rain is possible, so packing a light jacket or umbrella is advisable. The humidity is relatively comfortable, but bringing layers is a good idea to adapt to the changing temperatures throughout the day.



Medication




A nurse will be in the school, during the competition. Treatment will be provided in case of illness.

If further treatment is needed, student will be moved to the hospital nearest to the school and risk assessment will be followed.
