Principal's Welcome

A very warm welcome, from all at BIS Hanoi, to everyone attending the FOBISIA Primary Mathematics Competition 2025. We are looking forward to welcoming you to our school campus as you connect and compete with other students from around the FOBISIA region.

This competition is designed to test your mathematical skills, creativity, and teamwork. We have prepared a range of challenging problems and activities that will stimulate your minds and inspire you to think outside the box. You have been selected for this competition because you have demonstrated a flair for mathematics.

This event will provide you with opportunities to enjoy yourselves and learn. Teachers, we appreciate your commitment to nurturing the mathematical talents of our students. Your guidance and support are invaluable, and we are confident that your students will make you proud. Students, we believe in your potential, and we encourage you to give your best effort.

Remember that this competition is not just about winning but about learning, growing, and making new connections with fellow members of our FOBISIA student community. While you are here at BIS, do take some time to enjoy a brief insight into life in Hanoi, Vietnam. Have a great time and go ahead and be the best you can be!

Best wishes,
RICHARD VAUGHAN
PRINCIPAL



The 2025 Primary Maths Competition will be hosted by British International School Hanoi. FOBISIA Schools are invited to express interest to join. Schools can bring up to 2 teams of year 5-6 students. The 2025 competition is being hosted at BIS Hanoi from 14th - 15th March. Students will have a variety of outcomes at the end of the event.

Academic: Students will be encouraged to deepen their understanding of mathematical concepts and problem-solving techniques.
Inspirational: Students will show an enthusiasm for mathematics through engaging and challenging activities that highlight its beauty and relevance.
Collaboration: Students will be provided opportunities to work together, share ideas, and learn from one another in a supportive environment.
Nurture Talent: We will recognise and celebrate talented individuals in mathematics, providing them with a platform to showcase their skills.
Global Citizens: Students will meet and interact with students from across Asia to develop a greater sense of international mindedness.
