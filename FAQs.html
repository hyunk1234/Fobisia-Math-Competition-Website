Frequently Asked Questions

FOBISIA math competition FAQs

Will transport be available from both locations of Silk Path and Thang Long Opera Hotels?

Yes, BIS Hanoi will be providing transport from all locations of recommended hotels

Our flight won’t depart until Sunday morning, is this a problem?

No, this isn’t a problem. We will still provide transport to the airport on Sunday if you require this.

Is breakfast included in the hotel price?

Yes

What company are you using for your transportation?

We're using the same bus service provider for the event that we use for our daily student transportation and trips. This company has had an ongoing contract with BIS Hanoi since 2012. Please find their details below:
Company Name: Công ty TNHH Đầu Tư ABC Toàn Cầu Website: https://thuexedulich.com.vn/

Can parents be involved in the event?

Parents are welcome to attend the closing ceremony on Saturday. Due to safeguarding restrictions and in the interest of fairness, parents will not be able to attend the competition rounds. The event will be run by staff and students from BIS and with the assistance of teachers of visiting schools.
